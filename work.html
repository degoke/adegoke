Health & AI

Boolbyte

As founder, I am building toward an operating system for intelligent connected care and leading health AI research at Boolbyte — an AI operations layer that connects to existing clinical systems, runs healthcare workflows in the background, and surfaces only what needs human judgment across intake, scheduling, referrals, documentation, and follow-up.

An open-source modular foundation for FHIR-native tooling, structured AI workflows, sync engines, and local-first healthcare runtimes — the infrastructure layer I use to standardize data, connect systems, and bring intelligence into care environments.

Medplum

Open-source contributor to Medplum’s FHIR-native healthcare platform — work on the agent integration layer that connects clinical systems over MLLP, TCP, and related protocols.
