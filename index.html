What I Do

Work I am a senior backend and applied AI engineer, and a health AI researcher, building at the intersection of distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and digital healthcare. For five years at SkyeWallet, from founding engineer through lead engineer, I helped build and scale secure financial infrastructure — shaping high-concurrency systems built for reliability and transaction integrity at production scale. At Boolbyte, I am building toward an operating system for intelligent connected care and leading health AI research on clinical workflows, structured data, and applied intelligence in care environments. I also lead Healthcare Foundry Africa, where I support builders, students, and researchers across the African healthtech ecosystem through community, mentorship, and practical guidance. Projects My projects span open source foundations, cloud infrastructure, and tooling for structured health data and applied AI — work aimed at making complex systems easier to build, deploy, and trust in real-world environments. Health AI Stack is a modular open-source foundation for FHIR-native tooling, structured AI workflows, sync engines, and local-first healthcare runtimes. Geass brings managed cloud experiences to owned VPS or bare-metal infrastructure. Tronvent is a self-hosted TRON chain monitor that tracks wallet activity and delivers signed webhooks without requiring a full node. Writing I write practical notes and essays on backend systems, healthtech infrastructure, applied AI, and what it takes to build reliable software in production — especially where clinical data, payments, and intelligent workflows intersect.
